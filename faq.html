Everything you need to know about GIS 2025, tickets, venue, speakers, and working with Evenza.

Categories
📋 General
What is the Global Innovation Summit 2025?+

The Global Innovation Summit (GIS) 2025 is Evenza's flagship annual conference — a 3-day event bringing together 2,500 global leaders, entrepreneurs, investors, and innovators in Dubai. With 48 world-class speakers, workshops, panel discussions, and networking events, GIS 2025 is the year's most anticipated gathering for the global business and technology community.

When and where is GIS 2025 taking place?+

GIS 2025 takes place September 28–30, 2025 at the Dubai World Trade Centre, Sheikh Zayed Road, Dubai, UAE. The venue features multiple halls, workshop rooms, and event spaces all within one complex, making it easily navigable for attendees.

Who is this event for?+

GIS 2025 is designed for C-suite executives, entrepreneurs, investors, tech leaders, policy makers, and professionals across industries who want to stay ahead of global trends, build high-value connections, and gain actionable insights from the world's top minds.

How many people attended previous editions?+

GIS 2024 hosted 2,200 in-person attendees and was live-streamed to over 1.8 million viewers globally. GIS 2025 will be the largest edition yet, with capacity expanded to 2,500 in-person attendees and a global live stream available to registered virtual participants.

🎟️ Tickets & Registration
What ticket types are available?+

We offer three ticket tiers: Standard Pass (access to all keynotes, panels, and networking events), Premium Pass (Standard + workshops, gala dinner, and priority seating), and Elite Pass (Premium + VIP dinner, 1-on-1 speaker access, and exclusive lounge). See our Pricing page for full details and current rates.

Is there a group discount?+

Yes — groups of 5 or more receive a 15% discount, and groups of 10 or more receive 25% off. Contact our team at hello@evenza.com with your group size and preferred ticket type and we'll arrange your booking with the discount applied.

What is the refund policy?+

Tickets are fully refundable up to 60 days before the event. Between 30–60 days prior, a 50% refund is available. Within 30 days of the event, tickets are non-refundable but are fully transferable to another person. Simply contact us with the new attendee's details.

Will I receive a physical ticket?+

All tickets are digital. After registration, you'll receive a confirmation email with your QR code. Simply present this at registration on arrival and our team will check you in and provide your physical badge and event pack on the day.

📍 Venue & Logistics
How do I get to the Dubai World Trade Centre?+

The Dubai World Trade Centre is located on Sheikh Zayed Road and is accessible via Dubai Metro (World Trade Centre Station on the Red Line), taxi, or car. The venue has ample parking available for attendees driving. Detailed travel instructions and a venue map will be sent in your pre-event email.

Are hotel accommodations available nearby?+

Yes. We have negotiated preferential rates at several nearby hotels for GIS 2025 attendees, including properties ranging from 4-star to luxury 5-star. Details and booking links will be provided in your registration confirmation email.

Is the venue accessible for attendees with disabilities?+

Absolutely. The Dubai World Trade Centre is fully accessible with elevators, ramps, accessible restrooms, and reserved seating areas. If you have any specific accessibility requirements, please contact us at hello@evenza.com in advance so we can ensure the best possible experience for you.

🎤 Speakers
How are speakers selected for GIS 2025?+

Our curation team reviews hundreds of applications and nominations every year. We select speakers based on their relevance to our theme, depth of expertise, ability to deliver actionable insights, and the diversity of perspectives they bring.

Can I apply to speak at GIS 2025?+

Speaker applications for GIS 2025 are open until June 30, 2025. Submit your application via our Contact page with a brief bio, your proposed topic, and any previous speaking engagements. Our team will review and respond within 2 weeks.

Will I get access to session recordings after the event?+

Yes. All keynotes and panel sessions will be professionally recorded and made available to registered attendees within 2 weeks after the event. Premium and Elite Pass holders receive lifetime access to all recordings. Standard Pass holders receive 90-day access.

🌐 Hybrid & Online
Is there a virtual attendance option?+

Yes. GIS 2025 will be live-streamed globally via our proprietary hybrid event platform. Virtual passes are available at a reduced rate and include access to all live-streamed keynotes and panels, as well as the on-demand library post-event. Virtual attendees also have access to our digital networking lounge.

What technology will be used for the hybrid experience?+

We use our in-house platform combined with professional broadcast-grade production for all hybrid sessions. Virtual attendees can interact via live Q&A, polls, and our networking algorithm that matches you with other attendees based on your profile and interests.

⚙️ Working With Evenza
What types of events does Evenza manage?+

Evenza manages everything from intimate 50-person CEO dinners to 15,000-seat global summits. Our services include corporate conferences, product launches, gala dinners, hybrid events, exhibitions, award ceremonies, and government events. We've delivered 2,400+ events across 40+ countries.

How far in advance should I contact Evenza for my event?+

For large-scale conferences and summits, we recommend reaching out 9–12 months in advance. For mid-size events (200–1,000 attendees), 4–6 months is ideal. For smaller corporate events, 6–8 weeks is typically sufficient. Contact us and we'll let you know what's achievable.

Do you provide event management outside of the UAE?+

Absolutely. We operate globally with offices in Dubai, Singapore, London, New York, and Sydney, plus local partner networks in 40+ countries. We can manage events wherever you need them, with the same level of quality and service you'd expect locally.
