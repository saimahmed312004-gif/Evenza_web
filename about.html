Who We Are

A Legacy Built on
Passion & Precision

🌍
40+

2012
Founded

Evenza was founded in 2012 with a single, powerful belief — that every event, no matter the size, deserves to be extraordinary. What started as a small team of three passionate event professionals in Dubai has grown into a globally respected event management powerhouse.

Today, we operate across six continents, partnering with Fortune 500 companies, government bodies, tech giants, and cultural institutions to create events that don't just happen — they resonate, inspire, and transform the people who experience them.

From a 50-person CEO roundtable to a 15,000-seat international summit, we approach every project with the same relentless attention to detail, the same creative courage, and the same commitment to excellence that has defined us from day one.

Our Journey

12 Years of Milestones

2012
Founded in Dubai
Evenza was born in a small Dubai office with three co-founders, a laptop, and an unshakeable belief that events should be extraordinary. Our first client: a 200-person regional technology conference.
2015
First International Expansion
We opened our second office in Singapore, marking our entry into the Asia-Pacific market. That year, we delivered our 100th event and earned our first global industry award for innovation in event design.
2018
Landmark 10,000-Seat Summit
We produced the Global Future Leaders Summit in London — our largest event to date, with 10,000 attendees across 3 days, 120 speakers, and live-streamed to 2.3 million people worldwide. A defining moment in our history.
2020
Pioneering the Hybrid Era
When the world changed overnight, we led the industry's pivot to hybrid events. We launched our proprietary event technology platform, delivering over 300 hybrid experiences across 28 countries within 12 months.
2023
40+ Countries, 2,000+ Events
We crossed the milestone of 2,000 events delivered across 40+ countries, with offices in Dubai, Singapore, London, New York, and Sydney. Named Best Global Event Agency for the third consecutive year.
2025
The Next Chapter
We're expanding our AI-powered event planning tools, launching our sustainability certification program, and building toward our most ambitious year yet — with the Global Innovation Summit 2025 set to be our biggest event in history.
